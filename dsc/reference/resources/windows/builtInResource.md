* [组资源](groupResource.md)

* [注册表资源](registryResource.md)

* [服务资源](serviceResource.md)
* [用户资源](userResource.md)
* [WindowsFeature 资源](windowsfeatureResource.md)
* [WindowsProcess 资源](windowsProcessResource.md)
